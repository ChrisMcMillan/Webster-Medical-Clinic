Eugene McMillan, M.D. Internal Medicine

I graduated from Darthmouth Medical School. I have practice Internal Medicine in the Bay Area since 1981. Over the years I have worked in a variety of areas. I worked as a primary care internist for many years. I have also been extensively involved in acute hospital based medicine. I worked with the Adult Sickle Cell Program at Children's Hospital of Oakland for more than 10 years. During that period I also worked as an internist with the sub-acute rehab unit at Summit hospital. As a result I have extensive experience with post operative management of orthopedic procedures as well as patients recovering from cerebral vascular accidents and other acute medical illness. Most recently I have worked as a civil surgeon for the Immigration and Naturalization Service. I plan on continuing to work as a primary care physician for management of hypertension, ,diabetes,asthma sickle cell disease as well as other medical conditions.
